Welcome To Marlboro Trunk Or Treat!

Marlboro High School's Trunk or Treat event is a festive gathering where families decorate their car trunks and offer candy to children in a safe, community-oriented environment. This fun-filled evening promotes creativity and togetherness, making it a beloved tradition for students and residents alike.
